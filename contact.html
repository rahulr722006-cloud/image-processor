Contact Us

We’d love to hear from you. Whether you have questions, suggestions, feedback, or you want to report an issue, feel free to reach out anytime.

Email

You can contact us directly at:
studiohorizon.support@gmail.com

Why Contact Us?

You may contact us for:

  • Feedback about Compressly or our other tools
  • Bug reports or technical issues
  • Feature requests or improvements
  • General inquiries about Studio Horizon
  • Partnerships or collaborations

Response Time

We typically respond within 24–48 hours. If the message is regarding a technical issue, please include details such as:

  • What device/browser you used
  • Steps leading to the issue
  • Screenshots (if possible)

Follow-up

As Studio Horizon continues to grow, we’ll introduce more tools and features. Stay connected — we appreciate every message from our users!

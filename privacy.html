Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Overview

At Studio Horizon, your privacy is extremely important to us. Our tool Compressly is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or process your images on any server. Everything happens within your browser using local technology.

2. No Image Uploading

Compressly is a 100% client-side tool. - Your images never leave your device. - No files are sent to any server. - No copies are stored, logged, or transmitted. Once you close the page, all temporary data is lost.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do NOT collect any of the following:

  • Images or media files you select
  • Personal data or identity information
  • IP address or location
  • Image metadata or EXIF data
  • Usage logs tied to individuals

4. Cookies & Analytics

Compressly itself does not use cookies. However, if we use third-party services like Google Analytics or Google AdSense in the future:

  • They may use cookies to measure traffic or show ads
  • These cookies will never access your images
  • You can disable cookies from your browser settings at any time

5. Advertisements

If ads are shown via Google AdSense, Google may use cookies to personalize content. This does not affect your images in any way.

6. External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: studiohorizon.support@gmail.com (or your preferred email)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
